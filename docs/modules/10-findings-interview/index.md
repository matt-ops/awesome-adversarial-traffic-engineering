# Module 10 - Findings, briefing, and interview practice

**Outcome:** turn offensive evidence into an actionable finding, an exact retest,
a concise technical briefing, and a public-safe demonstration of engineering judgment.

## Foundation

Complete [finding and evidence](01-finding-and-evidence.md) and [the public-safe
role narrative](04-role-narrative.md). Produce one evidence chain and a truthful
90-second role explanation.

## Applied

Complete [remediation and exact retest](02-remediation-and-retest.md). Convert the
failed assumption into an invariant and a negative/positive acceptance matrix.

## Integrated

Complete [the technical briefing](03-technical-briefing.md). Expand the finding
to a three-to-five-page report and deliver the timed five-minute evidence narrative.

## Deep

Complete [the full mock loop](05-mock-loop.md), including rebuttal, exact gap
repairs, repeated failed sections, and a public-safe portfolio limitation statement.
Then complete the [Integrated and Deep portfolio package](../../labs/deep/portfolio-drills.md).

Start with [Finding and evidence](01-finding-and-evidence.md).
