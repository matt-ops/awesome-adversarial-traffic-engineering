# Module 10 - Findings, briefing, and interview practice

**Outcome:** turn offensive evidence into an actionable finding, an exact retest,
a concise technical briefing, and a public-safe demonstration of engineering judgment.

| Depth | Lessons and evidence |
|---|---|
| Foundation | One evidence chain and a 90-second role narrative |
| Applied | Remediation acceptance criteria and concise briefing |
| Integrated | Three-to-five-page report and five-minute briefing |
| Deep | Full mock loop, rebuttal, exact retest, and portfolio limits |
