# Findings and retesting

A defensible finding separates observation from inference. Record scope, hypothesis, method, baseline, evidence, alternative explanations, impact, root cause, false-positive concern, recommendation, validation criteria, limitation, and retest.

Recommendations should be specific enough to own and measure. A retest repeats relevant baseline and adversarial cases, includes legitimate populations, defines success and regression thresholds, and can falsify the claimed improvement.
