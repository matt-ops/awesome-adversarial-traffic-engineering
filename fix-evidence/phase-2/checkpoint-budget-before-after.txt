Checkpoint budget calculation before and after Phase 2
======================================================

Before methodology
------------------

The old checkpoint pages did not have canonical IDs or machine-readable time.
For an exact reproducible lower bound, each linked module depth section was
expanded to the lessons it named, direct lesson links were included, duplicate
lessons were counted once, and the visible lesson estimates were summed.
Unestimated lab and portfolio pages were excluded, so the old real totals could
only be higher.

Before results
--------------

- 24-hour definition: 28 unique lessons; 3,895 minutes; 64 hours 55 minutes.
- 7-day cumulative definition: 38 unique lessons; 5,575 minutes; 92 hours 55 minutes.
- 21-day cumulative definition: 44 unique lessons; 6,835 minutes; 113 hours 55 minutes.
- 6-week cumulative definition: 47 unique lessons; 7,435 minutes; 123 hours 55 minutes.

After methodology
-----------------

`scripts/validate_curriculum.py` sums every required canonical lesson ID exactly
once, rejects time outside the manifest range, and separately validates the full
prerequisite closure for missing IDs, cycles, and later-depth additions. The
checkpoint pages display the same derived minute totals.

After results
-------------

- 24 focused hours: 10 lessons; 1,425 minutes; 23 hours 45 minutes.
- 7 days cumulative: 15 lessons; 2,385 minutes; 39 hours 45 minutes.
- 21 days cumulative: 25 lessons; 4,305 minutes; 71 hours 45 minutes.
- 6 weeks cumulative: 34 lessons; 6,105 minutes; 101 hours 45 minutes.

Honesty boundary
----------------

The displayed totals are the required checkpoint lesson selections. A learner
who has not completed a prerequisite must add its full canonical estimate. The
pages say this explicitly; checkpoint naming never absorbs unfinished
prerequisite time.
