# 6-week checkpoint

> Portfolio-ready and capable of independent ramp-up in the specialty.

This checkpoint adds the Deep section of every module around one original research question. It deepens the same path; it does not introduce a new advanced course.

## Expected effort

Complete the Deep additions by approximately week six. Scope original work tightly enough to reproduce and defend it.

## Gate questions

Use the [Deep gate](../gates/deep-gate.md). Can you form and defend an original question, compare alternatives, show reproducible evidence, explain limitations, teach the result, and describe a safe production-validation plan?

## Ready for

- A public-safe portfolio discussion
- Independent ramp-up, research planning, and evidence review
- Proposing—not assuming—production validation

## Not yet claiming

- That local findings automatically generalize to a commercial edge or global traffic population
- That a research prototype is a production detector or DDoS platform
