# Interview scorecard

Score each dimension 1–4 and cite evidence. A score is a practice diagnostic, not a hiring prediction.

| Dimension | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Correctness | Material errors | Partial model | Correct core model | Correct with calibrated nuance |
| Depth | Tool names only | One layer | Cross-layer mechanisms | Implementation-aware tradeoffs |
| Structure | Hard to follow | Some sequence | Clear assumptions and flow | Adapts cleanly under follow-up |
| Tradeoffs | One answer | Names a downside | Compares alternatives | Links choices to outcomes |
| Measurement | No metric | Generic metric | Appropriate metric and baseline | Population/error/uncertainty aware |
| Safety | Missing | Generic caution | Scope, caps, abort, recovery | Challenges unsafe premise |
| Communication | Vague | Understandable | Concise and evidence-based | Teaches without overclaiming |
| Ownership | Unclear “we” | Task execution | Personal decisions and results | Durable mechanism and influence |

Critical misses requiring remediation regardless of total: unsafe testing, fabricated experience, identity claims from a fingerprint, unsupported production claims, or refusal to update after contradictory evidence.
