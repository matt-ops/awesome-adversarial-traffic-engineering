# Leadership story worksheet

Prepare at least eight distinct stories. Use only sanitized details in public artifacts.

## Story fields

- Situation and stakes
- Your exact responsibility
- Constraints and missing information
- Alternatives considered
- Actions you personally took
- Technical detail and mechanisms
- Disagreement or stakeholder tradeoff
- Customer or user consideration
- Metrics and result
- What failed or surprised you
- What you learned and would change
- Likely skeptical follow-up

## Suggested evidence categories

1. Severe incident
2. Difficult detection
3. Ambiguous investigation
4. Disagreement with a senior stakeholder
5. Failed approach
6. Urgent action with incomplete information
7. Cross-team improvement
8. Mechanism or automation created
9. Changed mind after new evidence
10. Prevented recurrence

Avoid turning a team result into personal credit. Name your decisions and actions while preserving collaborators’ contributions.
