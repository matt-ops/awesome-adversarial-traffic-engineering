# First 90 days

This is a generic proposal; real priorities require authorized system and stakeholder context.

## Days 1–30: learn and reproduce

- Learn systems, safety mechanisms, owners, telemetry, and deployment paths.
- Review existing detections, findings, customer-impact measures, and incident mechanisms.
- Reproduce one prior finding in the approved environment and document gaps.

## Days 31–60: own a bounded task

- Agree on one scoped research question and success criteria.
- Build or improve a small, reviewed mechanism.
- Produce a finding or negative result and partner on remediation and measurement.

## Days 61–90: lead a small engagement

- Lead scope, experiment, evidence review, and handoff for a bounded engagement.
- Validate one defensive change and document a repeatable retest.
- Propose the next-quarter research backlog based on measured gaps.
