# Bot detection

- [OWASP Automated Threats to Web Applications](https://owasp.org/www-project-automated-threats-to-web-applications/) — `[L1 Foundation]` `[Required]` · Modules 2 and 4 · Official project. Connects controls to automated business outcomes. It matters because detection should start with what abuse is being reduced.
- [MDN Web APIs](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API) — `[L1 Foundation]` `[Recommended]` · Module 4 · Official reference. Describes browser-exposed signals. It matters for avoiding mythical or misnamed features.
- [Rebrowser Bot Detector](https://github.com/rebrowser/rebrowser-bot-detector) — `[L2 Applied]` `[Recommended]` · Module 4 · Research prototype. Inspectable automation checks. It matters for comparing a single check with layered evidence.
- [OpenWPM](https://github.com/openwpm/OpenWPM) — `[L3 Integrated]` `[Optional]` · Modules 3–4 · Research project. Measurement instrumentation and structured data collection. It matters for reproducibility and research design.
